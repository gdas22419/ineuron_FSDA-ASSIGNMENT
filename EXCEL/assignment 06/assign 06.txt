4. Make a list of different shortcut keys that are only connected to
formatting with their functions

01. Open the Format Cells dialog box.                           Ctrl+1
02. Format fonts in the Format Cells dialog box.                Ctrl+Shift+F or Ctrl+Shift+P
03.Move the selected cells.                                     Ctrl+X
04. Copy the selected cells.                                    Ctrl+C
05. Paste content at the insertion point,                       Ctrl+V
replacing any selection.
06. Italicize text or remove italic formatting.                 Ctrl+I or Ctrl+3
07. Bold text or remove bold formatting.                        Ctrl+B or Ctrl+2
08. Underline text or remove underline.                         Ctrl+U or Ctrl+4
09. Apply an outline border to the selected cells.              Ctrl+Shift+Ampersand sign (&)
10. Remove the outline border from the selected cells.          Ctrl+Shift+Underscore (_)
11. Apply the Currency format with two decimal                  Ctrl+Shift+Dollar sign ($)
12. Apply the Percentage format with no decimal places.         Ctrl+Shift+Percent sign (%)
13. Apply the Scientific number format with two decimal places  Ctrl+Shift+Caret sign (^)
14. Apply the Date format with the day, month, and year.        Ctrl+Shift+Number sign (#)
15. Open the Insert hyperlink dialog box                        Ctrl+K
